## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 File Data
- **Free Plan:** Files automatically deleted after 20 minutes
- **Paid Plan:** Files automatically deleted after 24 hours
- **Enterprise Plan:** Custom retention period (configurable)

### 6.2 Account Data
- **Active Accounts:** Retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Inactive Accounts:** May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity

### 6.3 Analytics Data
- **Free/Paid Plans:** Retained for 90 days
- **Enterprise Plans:** Custom retention period
- **Logs:** Retained for 30 days (free/paid) or custom period (enterprise)

### 6.4 GDPR Compliance
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 1 year
- **Consent Records:** Retained for duration of relationship plus 3 years
- **DPA Records:** Retained for duration of relationship plus 3 years

### 6.5 CCPA Compliance
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 3 years
- **Opt-Out Records:** Retained indefinitely
- **Disclosure Requests:** Retained for 45 days

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- You have the right to request deletion of your data

- Deletion completed within 30 days

**Right to Restrict Processing:**
- You have the right to restrict how we process your data
- Contact support@example.com

**Right to Data Portability:**
- You have the right to receive your data in portable format

**Right to Object:**
- You have the right to object to processing
- Contact support@example.com

**Right to Correct:**

## 8. Data Security

### 8.1 Encryption

**In Transit:**
- All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
- Secure headers implemented (HSTS, X-Frame-Options, etc.)
- No unencrypted data transmission

**At Rest:**
- Files encrypted with AES-256
- Database connections encrypted
- Encryption keys managed by cloud provider
- Regular key rotation

### 8.2 Access Controls

- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Principle of least privilege
- Multi-factor authentication available
- API key authentication
- JWT token-based sessions

### 8.3 Security Measures

- Regular security audits
- Penetration testing
- Vulnerability scanning
- Dependency monitoring
- Security incident response procedures
- DDoS protection via Cloudflare
- Web Application Firewall (WAF)

### 8.4 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
- We will notify California residents without unreasonable delay (CCPA requirement)
- We will provide information about the breach and steps to protect yourself
- We will cooperate with law enforcement as required
